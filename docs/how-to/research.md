## Frame answerable questions

State the decision the research must inform, the required freshness, acceptable source types, and what would change the recommendation. Separate repository-only questions from external or mixed questions.

## Search the internet for external scope

External and mixed research must use web or browser search. Prefer primary sources: official documentation, standards, laws, specifications, and original research. Compare publication date with the date an event or change actually occurred.

## Build an evidence inventory

For each material claim record source, access date, scope, confidence, conflicts, and limitations. Do not convert a search snippet into a fact when the underlying source is available.

## Connect evidence to delivery

Link research conclusions to the affected requirement, decision, design, test, or risk. The research artifact informs authority; it does not silently become an approved product decision.

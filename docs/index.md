    <span class="label">Why it exists</span>
    <h2>AI speed without delivery amnesia.</h2>
    <p>Every workflow leaves behind enough structured evidence for the next person—or the next assistant—to continue safely.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="principles-grid">
    <article class="principle-card" data-index="01">
      <span class="icon">↳</span>
      <h3>Enter anywhere</h3>
      <p>Start from notes, a requirement, a diff, a QA gap, or a release question. The navigator recommends the smallest safe next action.</p>
    </article>
    <article class="principle-card" data-index="02">
      <span class="icon">◎</span>
      <h3>Trace everything</h3>
      <p>Requirements, decisions, tests, tasks, evidence, and handoffs stay linked in repository-local Markdown and compact TOON.</p>
    </article>
    <article class="principle-card" data-index="03">
      <span class="icon">◇</span>
      <h3>Scale the rigor</h3>
      <p>Move quickly on low-risk work and add stronger gates when security, data, compliance, or rollout risk demands it.</p>
    </article>
  </div>
</section>

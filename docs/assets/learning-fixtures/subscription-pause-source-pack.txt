LEARNING FIXTURE — BUSINESS ANALYST PATH — SUBSCRIPTION PAUSE
Snapshot: LF-BA-2026-07-21

Accepted outcome:
  Eligible customers can temporarily stop the next renewal without closing
  their account.

Current policy extracts:
  P-01 Monthly subscriptions renew at 00:00 UTC on the renewal date.
  P-02 A pause request received before renewal prevents the next charge.
  P-03 Annual plans and accounts with an unpaid balance are not yet covered by
       an accepted pause policy.
  P-04 Marketing notifications are optional; legally required billing notices
       are not classified in this fixture.

Current workflow observation:
  Support agents manually add a note and ask Billing Operations to stop renewal.
  The observation does not prove that the manual process is desired future policy.

Known source owners:
  Product owns intended customer outcome and priority.
  Billing Operations owns current execution evidence.
  Finance/Policy owns charge and refund policy.
  Security owns authorization requirements.

Learner boundary:
  Derive supported rules, exceptions, assumptions, and questions. Do not invent
  annual-plan behavior, refund rules, notice obligations, or authorization policy.
